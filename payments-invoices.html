Payment Application Submission

Follow the checklist below before submitting any payment application or proforma invoice.

Checklist
  1. The Subcontractor Agreement (ScA) and/or Subcontract Order (ScO) is in place.
  2. The subcontractor has established bank details and trade license details with the Procurement and Accounts Team.
  3. The subcontractor insurances required under the ScA/ScO are in place and still valid.
  4. The subcontractor bank guarantees or security cheques required under the ScA/ScO are in place and still valid.
  5. The application is submitted as per the agreed date and duration under the ScA/ScO.
  6. Submit the Payment Application / Proforma Invoice first. Submit the Tax Invoice only after issuance of the Payment Certificate (PC), unless otherwise agreed in advance.
  7. Attach all required approved Work Inspection Requests (WIRs) for the progress claimed.
  8. Attach all required approved Material Inspection Requests (MIRs) for Material on Site (MOS) claimed, together with Delivery Orders (DOs) signed by the contractor's authorized personnel.
  9. All MOS claimed must be supported with the material tax invoice.
  10. Attach highlighted approved shop drawings relevant to the works claimed.
  11. Attach the monthly progress report and updated programme, if contractually required.
  12. Attach updated variation logs and approved variation orders, if any.
Process
  1. The Payment Application shall be submitted on or before the contractual cut-off date.
  2. Submissions shall be made through the approved project document control system and addressed to the contractor's authorized representative.
  3. Late submissions shall not be entertained and may be automatically deferred to the next payment cycle.
  4. The subcontractor must ensure all required documents in the checklist are attached with the Payment Application / Proforma Invoice.
  5. The contractor may review quantities, compliance, and documentation and, where applicable, submit the same to the Engineer, Consultant, Employer, or Client for further review and assessment.
  6. Any discrepancies or non-compliance shall be formally returned to the subcontractor for correction and must be addressed within 24 hours. Any delay may be automatically deferred to the next payment cycle.
  7. Payment certification shall proceed only once all deficiencies are rectified and/or approved by the Engineer, Consultant, Employer, or Client, as applicable.

Important: Any direct tax invoice submitted before issuance of the Payment Certificate may be rejected unless agreed in advance.

Bank Guarantees Process

Ensure all guarantees, security cheques, and bank letters follow the agreed contractual requirements.

Checklist
  1. Verify the type of bank guarantees and/or security cheques required under the Subcontractor Agreement (ScA) and/or Subcontract Order (ScO).
  2. Check whether bank guarantee formats are provided, agreed, and whether their respective validity periods are defined and agreed.
  3. All original bank guarantees must be issued by a UAE Central Bank approved bank.
  4. All original bank guarantees, security cheques, and bank letters must be submitted to HO.
Submission Requirements
  1. Guarantee wording and validity shall strictly follow the prescribed format agreed within the ScA/ScO.
  2. All original guarantees shall be submitted to the contractor's Head Office, with copies to the site office.
  3. No Payment Certificate shall be processed unless valid guarantees are submitted, verified, and accepted in writing.
  4. The subcontractor is solely responsible for monitoring expiry dates, and extensions must be submitted not less than 30 days prior to expiry.
  5. Failure to extend guarantees may result in payment suspension or encashment.

Important: No payment certification can proceed unless the required guarantees remain valid and accepted in writing.

Process for Account Detail / Company Name Change

Changes to account details or company name must follow the formal approval path before any payment update is implemented.

Formal Request Submission
  1. Any change to bank account or company name details shall be requested through an official signed letter on the original company letterhead, signed by the authorized representative.
  2. Requests via informal email or verbal communication shall be rejected.
Supporting Documentation
  1. Updated bank confirmation letter (original).
  2. Valid trade license copy.
  3. Authorized signatory letter.
  4. Emirates ID and passport copies of authorized signatories, if requested.
  5. Emirates ID copy of the person submitting the original request to HO.
Verification Period
  1. The contractor reserves the right to independently verify the information with the bank and relevant authorities.
  2. No payment shall be processed to new accounts or new company details until the revised details are approved.
  3. If required, an amendment to the ScA/ScO will be issued for the changes and must be executed before implementing any update.
Risk and Liability
  1. The subcontractor shall undertake to amend or issue new insurances and bank guarantees due to the change in account details or name change, as applicable.
  2. The subcontractor bears full responsibility for delays caused by incomplete or incorrect submissions.

Important: Unapproved account or company detail changes will not be processed for payment.

Trade License Requirements

A valid trade license matching the contractual entity is mandatory throughout the contract period.

Validity and Compliance
  1. A valid trade license matching the contractual entity name is mandatory as referred to within the ScA/ScO.
  2. Licenses must cover the scope of works being executed under the contract.
Submission and Renewal
  1. Copies must be submitted at contract commencement and upon renewal.
  2. Expired trade licenses will immediately affect payment processing.
Impact of Non-Compliance
  1. Payments shall be suspended if the trade license expires.
  2. Continued non-compliance may result in contractual remedies as per contract conditions.

Important: Payments cannot be processed while the trade license is expired or non-compliant.

Payment Status Tracking

Use the following workflow to understand the current payment stage.

Payment Status Workflow
  1. Submitted to QS
  2. Certified by QS
  3. Submitted to Finance
  4. Processed by Finance
  5. Payment Done

Important: Payment status should be read in sequence from submission through payment done.

Supporting Documents (MIR / WIR)

Supporting document requirements depend on whether the request is commercial or QA/QC related.

Commercial and QA/QC Direction
  1. For commercial submissions, refer to the Payment Application Process and attach the required MIRs and WIRs as applicable.
  2. If the request is intended for the QA/QC process, approach the QA/QC Team and obtain their specific requirements directly.

Important: Missing approvals or using the wrong submission path will delay processing.
